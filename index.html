A Collection of Memories

The Story of Kho Tàng

According to local stories, a traveler once journeyed across Central Vietnam collecting things that could never be bought.

Not gold. Not jewels. Not hidden treasures.

Years later, his journal was divided into five chapters of discovery.

Before he left, he added one final blank page — not for himself, but for future travelers.

Today, those chapters are shared with explorers from around the world.

Questions

Frequently Asked

Do I need to complete chapters in order?

No. Explore freely. Each chapter is independent, and there is no required sequence.

How many missions must I complete per chapter?

One mission per chapter is sufficient to unlock its Archive Record. You are welcome to complete more.

How do I receive my Archive Code?

Present your completion photo at a participating accommodation.

Staff will provide an Archive Code for that chapter.

Enter the code on this website to unlock the corresponding Archive Record.

How do I receive my physical collectible?

After unlocking the Archive Record online, return to any participating accommodation and show the unlocked record screen to receive your physical reward.

What happens after completing five chapters?

Travelers who complete any five chapters will unlock the Collector's Letter.

The sixth chapter is optional and allows explorers to leave their own page in the archive.

Will my unlocked records be saved?

Yes. Your unlocked records are saved to this device. They will remain accessible when you return, as long as you use the same browser.
